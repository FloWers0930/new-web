About Shiromac Machine Shop

Our Story

Founded in 2024 in Bulacan, Shiromac Machine Shop began as a small family business with a passion for mechanical craftsmanship. Over the years, we've grown into a premier provider of CNC machining, custom fabrication, and engineering services. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation for reliability and precision.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional machine shop services that empower our clients to innovate and succeed, while always maintaining the highest standards of accuracy, safety, and integrity.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on precision and durability.
  • Service: Every customer is a partner in success.
  • Innovation: We harness the latest technology and techniques.
  • Community: Supporting local industries and giving back to Bulacan.
